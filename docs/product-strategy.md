# Cureomax Product Strategy

## Vision

Cureomax becomes the AI clinic operating system for Indian specialty clinics — the invisible backbone that converts inquiries, retains patients, and saves doctor time.

## Positioning

**Not**: A hospital management software  
**Not**: A telemedicine app  
**Not**: An AI doctor  

**Yes**: An AI clinic assistant — the operating layer between the doctor and the patient's journey

## Primary Market

Indian specialty clinics (private, 1–10 doctors):
- Physiotherapy
- Orthopedics
- Gynecology / Women's health
- Dental
- Dermatology
- Diabetes & chronic disease management

**First niche (pilot)**: Physiotherapy & orthopedic clinics in Delhi NCR

**Why physio/ortho first**:
- Repeat visits create measurable retention value
- Session packages create measurable billing value
- Pain score tracking creates measurable clinical value
- Drop-off problem is acute and well-understood by clinic owners
- Easy to demonstrate ROI in 14 days

## Core Problem Hierarchy

1. **Revenue leakage** — inquiries not converting, patients not returning
2. **Operational dependency** — systems depend on staff memory
3. **Doctor time waste** — repetitive documentation
4. **Owner blindness** — no visibility into clinic performance

## Product Module Priority

| Priority | Module | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | WhatsApp Inquiry Capture | First touchpoint — highest conversion impact |
| 2 | Patient CRM | Foundation for everything else |
| 3 | Follow-up Automation | Highest retention impact |
| 4 | Doctor Copilot | Doctor time saving — highest trust-builder |
| 5 | Billing | Revenue tracking |
| 6 | Analytics | Owner visibility |

## AI Safety Architecture

Every AI feature in Cureomax follows the **Doctor-in-the-Loop** principle:
- AI generates → Doctor reviews → Doctor approves → System acts

This is not optional. It is architectural.

No AI output (note drafts, summaries, reminders, communications) bypasses the doctor approval gate for clinical content.

## Revenue Model

- **Starter**: ₹4,999/month (small clinics)
- **Pro**: ₹14,999/month (most clinics)
- **Growth**: ₹29,999/month (multi-doctor)

**Pilot strategy**: 5 pilot clinics → proof-of-value → scale to 50 → 500

## 90-Day Pilot Goals

- 5 clinics onboarded
- Track: inquiries recovered, follow-ups completed, repeat visits
- Goal per clinic: demonstrate ≥ ₹15,000 in recovered revenue
- Convert pilots to paid at end of 14 days
