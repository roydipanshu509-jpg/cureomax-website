# Cureomax Founder Sales Script
_For Dipanshu — Delhi NCR clinic outreach_

---

## 30-Second Pitch

"Doctor, we built Cureomax for clinics that are losing patients between WhatsApp inquiries, missed follow-ups, manual notes, and no-shows. Cureomax works like an AI clinic assistant. It helps your team respond to patient inquiries, book appointments, create doctor-approved visit notes, send follow-up reminders, track payments, and show clinic analytics — all from one dashboard. The AI does not replace the doctor. It only helps your clinic save time and bring patients back."

---

## 2-Minute Pitch

"Most clinics do not lose patients because the treatment is bad. They lose patients because follow-up is weak, staff are overloaded, WhatsApp becomes messy, and doctors spend too much time on repetitive documentation.

Cureomax solves this by becoming your clinic's AI operating layer.

When a patient messages on WhatsApp, Cureomax captures the inquiry, understands the problem, creates an intake profile, and helps your receptionist book the appointment.

During or after the consultation, the doctor can use Cureomax to draft clinical notes and patient-friendly summaries.

After the visit, Cureomax automatically reminds the patient about medicines, exercises, reports, payments, and revisit dates.

The clinic owner gets a dashboard showing inquiries, appointments, revenue, no-shows, and follow-up recovery.

The goal is simple: save doctor time, reduce missed patients, and increase repeat visits."

"Doctor, before I show the product, I want to understand your current process.

- How do patients usually contact you — calls, WhatsApp, walk-ins, or Practo?
- Who handles follow-ups?
- How do you track no-shows?
- Do you currently know how many patients were supposed to come back but didn't?"

---

## Objection Handling

### "We already use WhatsApp."
"That makes sense, Doctor. Cureomax does not replace WhatsApp. It organises WhatsApp. The problem is not sending messages — the problem is tracking which patient needs follow-up, who has paid, who missed the appointment, who needs a revisit, and which inquiries did not convert."

### "AI is risky in healthcare."
"You're absolutely right, Doctor. That is why Cureomax is designed as an assistant, not a doctor. It does not make final diagnosis or send prescriptions by itself. It drafts notes, reminders, and summaries. Your approval remains mandatory."

### "My staff can do this manually."
"Yes, but manual follow-up usually breaks when the clinic gets busy. Cureomax gives your staff a system: pending follow-ups, missed patients, reminders, billing, and next actions all in one place. It reduces dependency on memory."

### "We don't have budget for this."
"Doctor, I understand. That's why we offer a 14-day pilot. No upfront cost to try it. We set everything up, customise the templates for your clinic, and you track three numbers: missed inquiries recovered, follow-ups completed, and repeat visits. If it doesn't create value, you don't continue."

### "I need to discuss with my staff."
"Absolutely, Doctor. Would it help if we did a quick 10-minute walkthrough with your reception staff as well? The system is designed to reduce their workload, not add to it. That usually answers most questions."
